About ARR Hospital

Caring for mothers and children with compassion, comfort and expertise

ARR Hospital is a specialized healthcare center in Tuticorin dedicated to the well-being of women and children. With a strong commitment to safety, comfort, and clinical excellence, we support families at every stage—from pregnancy to child development.

Under the leadership of Dr. S. Rajeswari, MD (OG), our hospital combines modern healthcare with a gentle, human touch. From the joy of pregnancy to the milestones of childhood, we stand by your side with care you can trust.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

At ARR Hospital, our commitment to women's and children's healthcare is guided by a clear mission and an inspiring vision for the future.

Our Mission

To provide safe, compassionate and high-quality healthcare for women and children, ensuring every patient receives personalized attention at every stage of their journey.

We are committed to combining clinical excellence with a warm, supportive environment where families feel confident and cared for — from the first consultation to every milestone that follows.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred hospital for women's and children's healthcare in the region — known for our expertise, integrity and patient-first approach.

We envision a future where every mother and child has access to world-class medical care delivered with empathy and advanced medical practices, setting new benchmarks in healthcare excellence.

ARR Hospital provides trusted and compassionate care for women and children, ensuring safe motherhood and healthy childhood at every stage.
